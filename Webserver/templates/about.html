MyShelter is a pet management RFID system that keeps track of adoptable dogs. Upon being surrendered to a shelter, each dog is given a unique RFID tag. The tag's ID is scanned into a database using our hand-held RF scanner. The ID is paired with the dog's photo, and placed on our adoption page. Once a dog is adopted, their tag is scanned again and their picture is automatically removed from our website.
